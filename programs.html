One of our Unique programs is caring for the elderly. We run foster homes that take care of the elderly and neglected peeople in the society. This could be widows,
widowers, and homeless people who have lost their families due to war, conflicts or death of family members.
We rely on your goodwill and support to keep this people comfortable.


Feel free to drop your donations.You can either donate cash, food stuffs or clothing.
or even your time. Your donations will help in a big way to the elderly.
We are commited to work togther with you to change elderly'life forever.
Old age doesn't have to be a curse.
As a community we came together and created a good environment for them to live in,
but our finances are not enough to maintain them so we are kindly requesting for your support.
We will be glad to be with you as we support them.
