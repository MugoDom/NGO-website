OUR CORE MISSION

NGO is a grassroot movement that majors in urban slums by providing very wanting services for all.We are committed in helping the society by providing education for the needy,skills development for our youths and care for the elderly and the homeless.

VISION

"Building everyone to be a better person all round."

WHO WE ARE.

The NGO is a non-governmental organization that is based Nairobi,Kenya along Argwings Kodhek road near Yaya Center.This NGO was founded by some members who are Domnic Mugo, Japheth Nyaranga,Mercy Vugutsa,Laurine Praise and Brian Obiri in the year 2020.

WHAT WE DO

We run several major projects that work in handy to lead to the promotion of education,skills development,care for the elderly and moreso for the homeless.

HOW WE DO IT.

For our work to be a success in years to come,forming a good rapport with various partners and relationship with various partners and donors is the major issues that we always want to achieve and maintain it for a successful goal.

OUR STORY

Seeing many children,youths,aged people and the homeless out there who are struggling with life and some social evils were increasing among the youths due to idleness we decided to come up with this project of helping out the needy so as to help communities out there. We decided to look for donors and partners to help us manage some things in terms of financial aid.We planned this for a very long time and we thank everyone who made all this a success in every way.This NGO was formed in the year 20202 during to this corona pandemic.
